History

Dragon: A Versatile Spacecraft

The Dragon spacecraft, developed by SpaceX, has a remarkable history in space exploration. It was designed to deliver cargo and humans to orbiting destinations, making space travel more accessible and efficient. Discover its achievements and capabilities below.

History and Configurations:

Commercial Resupply Services (CRS) to the ISS

  • CRS-11: On June 3, 2017, the CRS-11 capsule, largely assembled from previously flown components from the CRS-4 mission in September 2014, was launched again for the first time. It demonstrated the reusability of Dragon, with several components replaced.
